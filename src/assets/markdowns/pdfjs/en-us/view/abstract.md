## Attributes

 - **control*** : Sets the `pdfjsGroupControl` or the` pdfjsControl` that will provide the selected page to display
 - **fit** : Define in which direction the page will be fitted
 - **scale** : Allows you to define the scale, after having been previously fitted as defined by the `fit` attribute
